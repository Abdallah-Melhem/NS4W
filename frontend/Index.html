AI-Powered Genetic Disease Detection

GenDetect AI is an intelligent decision-support platform designed to assist in the early identification of potential genetic disorders. By combining machine learning models with structured medical data, the system analyzes key clinical and hereditary factors to generate predictive insights that can help guide further medical evaluation.

Through an AI-powered chatbot, users can interact naturally by providing health-related information step by step. The chatbot ensures required data is collected accurately, highlights missing details, and transforms user input into meaningful predictions, making complex medical data easier to understand for non-specialists.
